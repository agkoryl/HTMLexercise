Techniques

Painting
Painting is the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface (support base).
The medium is commonly applied to the base with a brush, but other implements,
such as knives, sponges, and airbrushes, can be used. Source
Sketch
A sketch is a rapidly executed freehand drawing that is not usually intended as a finished work.
A sketch may serve a number of purposes: it might record something that the artist sees,
it might record or develop an idea for later use or it might be used as a quick way of graphically
demonstrating an image, idea or principle.Source
Scrapbboking
Scrapbooking is a method of preserving, presenting, arranging personal and family history in the form of a book, box, card.
Typical memorabilia include photographs, printed media, and artwork.Source
